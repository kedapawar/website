Eligibility criteria for Second Year Diploma admissions:

1. 45% marks in HSC(12th) Science Stream & 2 Years ITI Completed Only
2. The Candidate should be an Indian National.
3. Backward Class categories candidates belonging to Maharashtra State.
4. Details regarding first year diploma admissions for the year 2022-23 will be made available by Admission Regulating Authority on the website: http://www.dte.org.in/
