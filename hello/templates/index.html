!!---OUR DEPARTMENTS---!!

COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY
Computer technology is simply the branch of science and engineering that deals with the creation and use of computers, as well as computer software development.

CIVIL ENGINNERING
Civil engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams etc.

ELECTRICAL ENGINNERING
Electrical engineering discipline concerned with the study, design, and application of equipment, devices, and systems which use electricity, electronics.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING
Mechanical engineering is an engineering branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics principles with materials science, to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.

ELECTRONICS & TELECOMMUNUCATION
Electrical engineering which emerged in the early 20th century and is distinguished by the additional use of active components such as semiconductor devices to amplify and control electric current flow.

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, storage, networking and other physical devices, infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, secure and exchange all forms of electronic data.
